About Me

Hi, my name is Scott Withers Jr.

Im an aspiring data scientist living in Charlotte, NC. I Like to work on projects that challenge my knowledge of statistics and understanding of visual learning. I enjoy working on both, solo and team oriented projects. The purpose of this site is to create a professional hub where all the things that I’ve done, am doing, and want to do can live. I don’t just want this place to be about me though; it’s also about you. I’m putting it out there on the interwebs, rather than boring paper copies (what year is it?), because I want to work with you.

If you are not familiar with github pages, everything you see here, whether good or bad, I hand-coded. I choose to forego content management and hosting services like Wix, Weebly, or SquareSpace and do it myself. I hope you enjoy it and if you have any questions or wish to talk further please feel free to contact me.
